## I am dedicated to designing experiences that align with people’s values and aspirations.

## I aim to create down-to-earth solutions that cross traditional boundaries of mediums, delivering holistic experiences and engaging service designs.

Approach

## From identifying problems to crafting solutions, my works explore how design can serve as a communication tool between designers and users, based on keen observation and communication that uncover what we truly want and need.
