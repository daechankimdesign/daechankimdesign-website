A weekend experiment: a small, fast interactive map that surfaces nearby parks
and a little story about each one — trails, hours, what makes them worth the walk.

![Park map interface](https://picsum.photos/seed/park-ui/1600/900)

## What it does

Pan and zoom to a neighborhood; parks animate in with a short blurb and a photo.
The goal was discovery, not navigation — fewer pins, more reasons to go outside.

## Notes

Built quickly with React, Framer Motion for the transitions, and Claude Code for
the data wrangling. A sandbox piece — rough edges left intentionally visible.
