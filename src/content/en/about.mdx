Originally from South Korea and trained in the United States as an industrial designer, he expresses a unique balance of restraint and clarity through honest materiality and unpretentious form.

Grounded in close observation of human behavior, his practice transcends the traditional boundaries of product, service, and experience design, distilling complexity into clear, intuitive solutions.

Approaching design as a foundational language, he creates objects that genuinely invite interaction, bringing function, human perception, and emotion into quiet alignment.

From identifying problems to crafting solutions, his work explores how design can serve as a communication tool between designers and users, grounded in keen observation that uncovers what people truly want and need.
