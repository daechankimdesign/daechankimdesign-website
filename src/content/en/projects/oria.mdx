## The problem

More content is created and consumed digitally, yet older generations are often left behind — widening the gap of family stories, local histories, and cultural events that go unrecorded.

## The solution

Oria lets anyone record personal stories and share them with connected family and friends, using pre-populated questionnaires and conversational AI to lower the psychological barrier to sharing.

## Designs

Built on three pillars — simplicity, empathy, and clarity — the redesigned system reduces color and typographic noise to strengthen contrast and information hierarchy.
